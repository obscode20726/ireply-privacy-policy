iReply Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to iReply, an AI-driven Instagram comment reply automation service. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use iReply, we collect:

  • Instagram account information (username, profile data)
  • Instagram access tokens for authentication
  • Comments and interactions from your Instagram posts
  • AI-generated responses and interaction history

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide automated AI-powered comment responses
  • Improve our AI response accuracy
  • Maintain and enhance service functionality
  • Ensure service security and prevent misuse

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure data storage practices
  • Regular security audits

5. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

  • Instagram API (Meta)
  • AI language processing services

Usage of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Opt-out of data collection
  • Revoke Instagram access permissions

Email: ireply.app@gmail.com

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy as our service evolves. Users will be notified of significant changes via email or service notifications.
